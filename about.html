Our Mission

To reunite alumni of Odogbolu Grammar School 2011/2012 set, fostering lifelong connections and professional growth through networking, mentorship, events, and mutual support.

Our Vision

To create a strong, united, and impactful alumni community that contributes to the success of its members and gives back to the school and society at large.

Our Core Values

  • Unity: We believe in the power of togetherness to strengthen our bonds.
  • Growth: We foster personal, academic, and professional development among members.
  • Support: We assist one another in career, business, and personal development.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, respect, and commitment in all our dealings.
  • Giving Back: We support Odogbolu Grammar School through initiatives like mentorship, donations, and community service.
