About OGS 2011/2012 Alumni

Building a strong community through connection, support, and shared experiences.

🌍 Who We Are

The OGS 2011/2012 Alumni Association is a dynamic network of past students committed to staying connected, fostering lifelong friendships, and contributing to the growth of our alma mater.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to reconnect, inspire, and support our members by organizing reunions, career mentorship programs, and social initiatives.

💡 What We Do

  • 📌 Organize reunions and networking events
  • 📌 Support alumni in career development
  • 📌 Engage in charity projects for the school
  • 📌 Provide mentorship for current students

🤝 Get Involved

Whether you want to attend an event, volunteer, or contribute ideas, there's a place for you in our community!
